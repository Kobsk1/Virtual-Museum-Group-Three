Meet the creators behind this virtual museum dedicated to Napoleon Abueva

Our Project

This virtual museum is a tribute to Napoleon Abueva, the Father of Modern Philippine Sculpture. Our goal is to make his remarkable artworks and legacy accessible to everyone through this digital platform.

We've carefully curated his most significant sculptures and provided detailed information about his life, achievements, and impact on Philippine art.

This project was created as part of our Art Appreciation subject to showcase how technology can help preserve and promote cultural heritage.

Art Appreciation Project

This virtual museum was created as a midterm project for our Art Appreciation subject. Our goal was to apply our understanding of art history, aesthetics, and digital presentation to create an engaging educational resource about one of the Philippines' most important artists.

Through this project, we've learned to appreciate the significance of Napoleon Abueva's contributions to Philippine art and how digital platforms can help preserve cultural heritage for future generations.
